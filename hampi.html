Hampi Heritage Tour

Tour Overview

Step into a world of history, grandeur, and timeless beauty at Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore ancient ruins, majestic temples, and the captivating boulder-strewn landscapes that narrate the glory of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Tour Inclusions

  • Pickup & Drop from Bengaluru (Sleeper Bus)
  • 2 Days Guided Heritage Tour
  • Stay at Comfortable Heritage Hotel (Twin Sharing)
  • Breakfast & Dinner (Veg/Non-Veg)
  • Professional Guide and Entrance Fees
  • Evening Cultural Walk & Sunset Point Visit
  • First Aid Kit and Tour Manager Support

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1 Night: Departure from Bengaluru by Sleeper Coach
  • Day 2 Morning: Check-in and Breakfast
  • Day 2: Full-day Guided Tour (Virupaksha Temple, Vittala Temple, Stone Chariot, Hampi Bazaar)
  • Day 2 Evening: Sunset at Hemakuta Hill, Cultural Exploration
  • Day 3 Morning: Visit Anjanadri Hill (Birthplace of Hanuman)
  • Day 3 Afternoon: Leisure Time for Shopping & Local Exploration
  • Day 3 Evening: Return Journey to Bengaluru
