Bengaluru to Murudeshwara Tour

About the Journey

Experience the breathtaking drive from Bengaluru to Murudeshwara along NH 75 and NH 66, passing through the scenic landscapes of Dakshina Kannada. Explore coastal beauty, ancient temples, and the second tallest Shiva statue in the world!

Highlights of the Tour

  • Murudeshwara Temple and Giant Shiva Statue
  • Beautiful drive through Charmadi and Shiradi Ghats
  • Visit Udupi Krishna Temple
  • St. Mary's Island ferry trip (optional)
  • Beach walks and Sunset Views
  • Local Dakshina Kannada Cuisine Experience
  • Relaxing stays near the beach

Tour Inclusions

  • Pickup & Drop from Bengaluru (Sleeper A/C Bus)
  • Hotel Accommodation (Beach Resorts)
  • Guided Temple Visits
  • 2 Breakfasts + 2 Lunches + 1 Dinner (local delicacies)
  • Entry Tickets and Ferry Charges
  • Travel Insurance

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 1 Night: Depart Bengaluru (overnight journey)
  • Day 2 Morning: Arrival in Udupi - Visit Krishna Temple
  • Day 2 Afternoon: Explore beaches (Malpe Beach, St. Mary's Island optional)
  • Day 2 Evening: Reach Murudeshwara - Check-in Hotel
  • Day 3 Morning: Visit Murudeshwara Temple & Statue
  • Day 3 Afternoon: Return Journey to Bengaluru
