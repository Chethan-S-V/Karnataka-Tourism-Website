Gokarna Beach Trekking & Camping

Tour Overview

Escape to the pristine beaches of Gokarna for a magical experience of beach trekking and camping under the stars! Explore hidden beaches, trek along coastal cliffs, and relax with a campfire on the sand.

Tour Inclusions

  • Pickup & Drop from Bengaluru (Sleeper AC Bus)
  • Beachside Camping Tents
  • 2 Breakfasts, 1 Dinner (Veg/Non-Veg)
  • Experienced Trek Guide
  • Trekking and Camping Equipment
  • Campfire & Music Night
  • Basic First Aid Kit

Tour Itinerary

  • Day 0 (Night): Pickup from Bengaluru (9:00 PM) - Overnight Journey
  • Day 1: Arrival at Gokarna - Freshen Up - Start Beach Trek (Paradise Beach, Half Moon Beach, Om Beach) - Campfire & Camping
  • Day 2: Sunrise Beach Walk - Breakfast - Explore Gokarna Town - Departure to Bengaluru - Arrival by Night
