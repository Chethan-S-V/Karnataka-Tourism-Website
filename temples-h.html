Temples of Hampi Tour

About the Tour

Witness the architectural brilliance and spiritual heritage of Hampi's temples. Discover ancient shrines, majestic gopurams, intricate carvings, and the legendary stories carved in stone. A journey through the temples of Hampi is a journey through time itself!

Key Temples Covered

  • Virupaksha Temple (oldest functioning temple)
  • Vittala Temple (Stone Chariot & Musical Pillars)
  • Achyutaraya Temple (quiet ruins, grand entrance)
  • Hemakuta Hill Temples (for sunrise & sunset views)
  • Hazara Rama Temple (epic Ramayana carvings)
  • Krishna Temple (dedicated to Bala Krishna)
  • Underground Shiva Temple (mystic vibes)
  • Sasivekalu Ganesha & Kadalekalu Ganesha Temples
